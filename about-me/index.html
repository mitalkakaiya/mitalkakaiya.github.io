About Me

Mar 25th, 2014 | Comments

WHO ARE YOU?

I’m Mital Kakaiya, and am living in Melbourne, Australia.

WORK EXPERIENCE?

I am working as Technical Lead / Senior Software Developer in financial/accounting field. I have over 10+ years of experience in enterprise application development using Microsoft tools and technologies. Expert in .NET Framework, C#, VB.NET, ASP.NET, WCF, WPF, XML, SQL Server, Windows Azure (Cloud). Extensive experience in financial, accounting & ERP projects. Also, I have acquired Microsoft Certified Solution Developer (MCSD) for .NET Framework.

EDUCATION?

I have always been fascinated by computers, and since I was in school I knew I would end up in a career working with them. I completed my degree in Electronics and learnt internals of computer processors and their CPU activities to process instructions given by programming languages. To enhance my career in the computing field, I completed Master of Engineering in Information Technology from RMIT University, Melbourne.

WHY BLOG?

I read lots of books, programming blogs and technical articles daily, which helps me to learn new skills and explore ideas. I think this blog will help me to improve my skills further by sharing my thoughts & knowledge to everyone, and hopefully it will help others too. Also, Writing a blog entry is a challenge for me and a commitment to myself to keep thinking, questioning, and finding new insights that are worth sharing.
